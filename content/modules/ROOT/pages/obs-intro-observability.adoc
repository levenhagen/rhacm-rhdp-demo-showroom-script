== Concepts & Architecture

MultiCluster Observability, in the context of RHACM, represents the observability signals that provides insights about the health and utilization of clusters, and workloads across the entire fleet of clusters. Let's have a quick look at the architecture:

image::obs-observability-arch.png[RHACM Observability architecture]

In short, when the MultiCluster Observability operator resource is enabled and deployed, it's going to deploy these Open Source components in the Hub:

* https://grafana.com/oss/[Grafana] - for Dashboards visualization
* https://thanos.io/[Thanos] - for long-term storage and data retention
* https://prometheus.io/docs/alerting/latest/alertmanager/[AlertManager] - for handling and grouping alerts in a multicluster setting

As consequently, the operator will also deploy and manage collectors on the managed clusters, so you don't have to worry about managing any new stacks, deployments or configurations. RHACM supports the provided stack will take care of that part.
